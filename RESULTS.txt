## Analysis

### Key Findings

1. **BasicNBody Performance**: Still faster at 10K particles (2.09 sec vs 2.71 sec)
   - O(N²) algorithm with good cache locality wins
   - Simple loops optimize well

2. **Barnes-Hut Overhead**: Algorithm overhead dominates
   - Tree construction: O(N log N)
   - Octree memory allocation slower than expected
   - Crossover point: likely 50K+ particles

### Observations

- BasicNBody: ~9.59e+07 particles/sec
- Barnes-Hut: ~6.79e+04 particles/sec
- Speedup: 0.71x (BasicNBody faster)

Expected behavior:
- At 50K: Barnes-Hut should be ~3-5x faster
- At 100K: Barnes-Hut should be ~10-20x faster
